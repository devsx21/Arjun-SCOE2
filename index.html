What is GEV?

Govardhan Eco Village (GEV) is a sustainable community and retreat center located in Maharashtra, India. Founded by the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON), it serves as a model for eco-friendly living, spiritual practice, and sustainable development.

Read more

Vision and Mission

The village is guided by the principles of eco-friendly living, sustainable agriculture, and spiritual harmony. Its mission is to demonstrate and promote holistic, sustainable living practices that are in harmony with nature and supportive of individual and community well-being.

Read more

Sustainability

The community emphasizes eco-friendly practices such as organic farming, renewable energy use, waste management, and water conservation. They strive to minimize their ecological footprint and live in harmony with nature.
